Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

NVR Labs ("we", "our", "us") operates the application VR Quest (the "App") on Meta Quest. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our App.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The App does not require account registration or login. We do not collect names, email addresses, or other identifiers.

3. How We Use Information

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not use it for advertising, analytics, or any other purpose. Game progress and preferences are stored only on your device.

4. In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed through Meta's Digital Goods API. All payment and billing data are handled by Meta and are governed by Meta's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store your payment information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. We do not sell personal information.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Progress and settings are stored locally on your device. We do not retain data on our servers. If you uninstall the App, local data is removed with the App.

7. Analytics and Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Your Rights

You may request information about data we hold. Because we do not collect personal data, there is no account data to access, correct, or delete. For questions, use the contact details below.

10. International Data

We do not transfer or store personal data internationally; we do not collect personal data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect changes. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
