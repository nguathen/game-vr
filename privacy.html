Privacy Policy

VR Target Shooter — Last updated: January 29, 2026

1. Introduction

NVR Labs ("we", "our", "us") develops and publishes VR Target Shooter (the "App"), available on the Meta Quest Store. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

2. Data We Collect

We designed the App to minimize data collection. Specifically:

  • No personal information collected. We do not collect your name, email, address, or any other personally identifiable information.
  • No account required. The App does not require you to create an account or sign in.
  • No tracking or analytics. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.
  • No network data transmitted. The App does not send your gameplay data, device identifiers, or usage patterns to any external server.

3. Local Data Storage

The App stores game progress (scores, coin balance, purchased items) locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data:

  • Remains on your device only
  • Is not transmitted to us or any third party
  • Can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing browser data

4. In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Meta's commerce platform (Digital Goods API). When you make a purchase:

  • All payment processing is handled by Meta
  • We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information (credit card, billing address, etc.)
  • Your purchase is governed by Meta's Privacy Policy and Meta's Terms of Sale

5. Children's Privacy

The App is rated for Teens and Adults (ages 13+). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise share any user data with third parties, because we do not collect any user data.

7. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store personal data on our servers, there is no risk of a data breach from our side. Local game data on your device is protected by your device's own security measures.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal data we hold about you (we hold none)
  • Request deletion of your data (local data can be deleted by uninstalling the App)
  • Opt out of data collection (no data is collected)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
